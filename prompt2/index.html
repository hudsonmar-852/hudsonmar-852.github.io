Correct logic: the page now separates the input prompt into individual meaningful phrases, then groups them by impact to the subject.

例子：
English: Natural Sun-Kissed Tan
中文：自然陽光曬感健康古銅膚色

Meaningful Impact Groups

Instead of forcing the whole prompt into 4 long phases, phrases are grouped by their impact on the subject and production result.

1 · Identity / 保留主體

Phrases that protect face, body, pose, age and expression.

2 · Appearance / 外觀質感

Skin, physique, texture, tone and subject rendering.

3 · Visual / 視覺語言

Lighting, camera, ambience, colour science and commercial style.

4 · Avoid / 避免錯誤

Negative quality control phrases converted into clear restrictions.
